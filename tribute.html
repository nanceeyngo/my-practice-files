TRIBUTE TO A PRICELESS MUM

MAMA LIKE NO OTHER

To a self-sacrificing woman whose dedication to her family is evidenced by an unflinching perseverance, and written on the sands of time

A strong woman; hard-pressed, but not broken

Strengthened by the tide; toughened by the storms

A mother in word and deed; her love and care, covering as a shield

Unrelenting in affection; her heart, closely bound to those of her children

My Daddy's best friend; of course his girlfriend

A shoulder to lean on; a true friend in distress

A mother I would ask for over again; a most cherished asset, invaluable and irreplaceable

A mother of all, a mother for all

Thank you Mum for all you do.

You're indeed cherished!
